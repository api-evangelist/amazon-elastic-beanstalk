AWS Graviton5-based M9g database instances are now generally available for Amazon RDS PostgreSQL, MySQL, and MariaDB, providing up to 30% performance improvement and up to 23% price/performance improvement over Graviton4. New 24xlarge and 48xlarge sizes deliver up to 192 vCPU, 100Gbps networking, and 72Gbps EBS bandwidth, available in Ohio, Virginia, Oregon, and Frankfurt.
