Amazon SNS customers in the Asia Pacific (Seoul) Region can now broadcast text messages to subscribers across more than 200 countries and territories. Seoul Region users can subscribe phone numbers to SNS topics and use AWS End User Messaging for SMS distribution alongside delivery to Lambda, SQS, Data Firehose, mobile devices, and email.
