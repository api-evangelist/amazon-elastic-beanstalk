AWS makes the all-MiniLM-L12-v2 model from Sentence Transformers available in Amazon SageMaker JumpStart. The model converts sentences and paragraphs into 384-dimensional dense vectors for semantic search, text clustering, duplicate detection, and paraphrase identification, deployable through SageMaker Studio or the Python SDK.
