Amazon Aurora MySQL-Compatible Edition and Amazon RDS for MySQL now provide Extended Support for MySQL 5.7 through June 30, 2029, extending the previous February 28, 2027 end date. This applies to Aurora MySQL version 2 and RDS MySQL 5.7, continuing critical security patches and bug fixes at no price increase with Year 3 pricing through the extended deadline.
