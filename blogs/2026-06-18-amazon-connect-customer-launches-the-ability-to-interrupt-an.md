date: "2026-06-18"

---
Amazon Connect Customer adds contact interruption, allowing urgent communications to reach agents regardless of standard routing. An agent on a regular call can receive an urgent callback on their personal extension and choose to hold the first caller, and contacts can be assigned directly to agents even when they are in custom status modes that normally prevent queued delivery.
