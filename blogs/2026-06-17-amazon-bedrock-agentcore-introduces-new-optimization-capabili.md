Amazon Bedrock AgentCore adds optimization capabilities converting production traces into continuous agent improvement, surfacing failure, intent, and trajectory insights across hundreds of sessions. Recommendations suggest specific system prompt and tool description improvements, with batch evaluation against defined datasets and A/B testing to confirm improvements before fleet-wide commitment.
