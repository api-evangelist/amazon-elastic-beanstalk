AWS expands nested virtualization support to additional Intel instance families including C7i, R7i, M7i, C8id, R8id, M8id, flex variants, I7i, and X8i, plus US GovCloud East and West regions. Nested virtualization enables running KVM or Hyper-V on EC2 instances for use cases like mobile app emulators, vehicle hardware simulation, and Windows Subsystem for Linux.
