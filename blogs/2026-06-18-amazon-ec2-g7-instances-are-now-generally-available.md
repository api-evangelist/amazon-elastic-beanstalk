Amazon EC2 G7 instances, powered by NVIDIA RTX PRO 4500 Blackwell Server Edition GPUs, are now generally available, delivering up to 4.6x AI inference performance and up to 2.1x graphics performance versus G6. Configurations include up to 8 GPUs with 32 GB memory each, custom Intel Xeon 6 processors, and 700 Gbps Elastic Fabric Adapter networking, available in Ohio and Oregon.
