AWS Sign-in implements resource-based policies and resource control policies (RCPs) for the AWS Management Console, restricting console sign-in to expected networks. Policies evaluate during sign-in and whenever sessions request new credentials; resource-based policies apply to individual accounts while RCPs apply organization-wide, available at no additional cost across all commercial regions.
