Amazon S3 Vectors increases the maximum similarity search results to 10,000 per query, a 100-fold increase from previous limits, assisting multi-stage retrieval pipelines that apply reranking, aggregations, or deduplication. QueryVectors API responses deliver results across multiple pages, and the first 512 KB of returned data per query remains complimentary.
