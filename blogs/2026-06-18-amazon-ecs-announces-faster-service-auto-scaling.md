Amazon ECS service auto scaling now responds faster through high-resolution 20-second metrics and publishing optimizations. AWS benchmarking shows time to trigger scale-out improved from 363 to 86 seconds (76%) and total scaling and provisioning time reduced from 386 to 109 seconds (72%), with the high-resolution option available for CPU and memory target tracking policies.
