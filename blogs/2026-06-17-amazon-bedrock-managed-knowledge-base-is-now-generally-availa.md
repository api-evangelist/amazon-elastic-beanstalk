Amazon Bedrock Managed Knowledge Base, a fully managed retrieval-augmented generation service, reaches general availability for building production-ready AI agents grounded in enterprise data without vector database management. Six native connectors (Amazon S3, SharePoint, Confluence, Google Drive, OneDrive, and Web Crawler) provide automatic syncing, with hybrid search, document ranking, and agentic retrieval.
