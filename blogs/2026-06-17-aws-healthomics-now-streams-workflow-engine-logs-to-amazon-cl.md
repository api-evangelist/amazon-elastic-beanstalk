AWS HealthOmics enables real-time streaming of workflow engine logs to Amazon CloudWatch, letting customers observe workflow execution as it progresses. The HIPAA-eligible service delivers workflow orchestration events, task scheduling details, and complete stack traces on failures, and CloudWatch alarms can detect anomalies early.
