AWS Transform offers a model-to-model migration custom transformation that assesses generative AI workloads and produces migration plans for third-party-to-Bedrock transitions. AI-powered agents scan codebases identifying every AI SDK and model, then map models to Bedrock equivalents with cost comparisons; support extends to OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic SDK, and open-source models via LiteLLM or Ollama.
