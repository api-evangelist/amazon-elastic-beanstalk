AWS launched a new Local Zone in Hanoi, Vietnam, one of the first Local Zones in Asia Pacific offering Amazon S3 and Amazon EBS Local Snapshots support to meet data residency needs. The zone supports C7i, M7i, and R7i compute instances, storage, container services, and networking, and can be enabled from the AWS Global View or via API.
