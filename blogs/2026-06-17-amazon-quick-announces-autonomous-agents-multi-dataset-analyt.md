AWS unveils new capabilities for Amazon Quick, including autonomous agents that handle recurring tasks with granular autonomy levels, multi-dataset analytics enabling natural language querying across Snowflake and relational databases, and a redesigned activity feed. The feed offers a personalized conversational interface with thumbs up/down feedback, email and Slack replies, and direct request approvals.
